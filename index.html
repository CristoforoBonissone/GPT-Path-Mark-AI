About the project

Built to benchmark folders, not abstractions.

The idea

GPT Path Mark AI was created to measure storage performance directly where files are actually stored. The goal is to make benchmark results easier to understand and closer to everyday use.

The application uses a focused SEQ1M Q8T1 test and reports the best completed result.
